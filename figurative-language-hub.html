Figurative Language

Three activities that take you from sorting examples to writing your own. Work through them in order.

1
Learn & Sort
Activity 1: Sort and Identify

Match definitions to the six types of figurative language, sort 28 example sentences into categories, then write your own examples with AI feedback.

→
2
Spot & Build
Activity 2: In Action

Spot figurative language in a real passage, build sentences by dragging different endings onto stems, then write your own sentence for each type.

→
3
Write & Publish
Activity 3: The Workshop

Rewrite a plain paragraph using figurative language, free write your own piece packed with techniques, then publish it to a gallery card you can print.
